Usability Test Plan Section

Test Plan for: Product

Persona Type Tested: Primary/Secondary

Context: an environment (location) where the user is comfortable without distractions
Duration: 15 minutes

Complete the following before starting: Greeting and Consent & User Background Information.

Overall Objectives
  • Determine how intuitive the main flow of the application is.
  • Identify possible bottle-necks in the 'search product' workflow
  • Users shall be able to perform the task: 'search product'
  • Determine how quickly users find the results
Scenario

User is shopping online and finds a product that he might buy. He does not have too many insights about that vendor so he wants to verify the genuineness of the product. In addition he wants to find all the alternatives.

Tasks

Primary persona

  • Open the app - 3 seconds
  • Sign up - 10 - 30 seconds (depends on the method)
  • Search a product - 15 seconds
  • View & inspect results - 10 seconds
  • Go to a product page from the results - 5 seconds
  • Review the results - 3 seconds

Secondary persona

  • Open the app - 5 seconds
  • Sign up - 20 - 40 seconds (depends on the method)
  • Search a product - 20 seconds
  • View & inspect results - 15 seconds
  • Go to a product page from the results - 10 seconds
  • Review the results - 5 seconds
Post-test Questions

These questions could be presented to the user as a questionnaire or verbally asked.

  • Is 'search product' action easy to perform?
  • Would you prefer other methods for selecting the image (crop, copy-paste)?
  • How often would you rate a result?
Notes for Test Coordinator
  • Remind the user that you would like them to 'think aloud' so that you can understand what they are thinking, about the task or the product/application;
  • Read the user the scenario for the test;
  • Read the user the tasks he has to complete. If the tasks are complex, you may want to put a sheet of paper in front of the user with each task written out;
  • Record the user's actions, comments, questions, and body language (write them in the Test Observations section accompanied by video/audio).
  • Don't offer help; let the user attempt to perform the task. If they ask for help reply with: "What do you think you/that would do?" "What do you think that means?".

Test Results for: Product

Success Criteria

A successful design has been achieved when:

  • 100% of users searched a product successfully
  • 66% of users reached a product page from the results
  • 33% of users reviewed a result
  • 100% of primary users searched a product in under 15 seconds

Summary

  • 3 users found quickly how to perform a 'search product' task
  • 1 user did not find very fast the results.
  • 3 users reached a product page from the results
  • 3 users reviewed the results easily

A user might complete a task in a shorter or greater time than the estimated time. If the completion time exceeds the estimated time then it there is an issue with the design. Also a user might skip the current task thus the completion time would be 0.

Post-test Questions Answers

Is 'search product' action easy to perform? - Yes

Would you prefer other methods for selecting the image (crop, copy-paste)? - No

How often would you rate a result? - 50% of the time

Test Observations

  • User 1: he managed to accomplish all the tasks very fast; he understood the flow easily; he did not like that the results weren't very good (the product did not math); he would like to have a price convertor; he did not like the logo.
  • User 2: she understood the flow overall; she had some privacy concerns; results should auto-expand; she would like to mark results as favorite; she liked the review system; she liked the design and the colors;
  • User 3: she is not an expert in technology; it took some time to sign-up but she did it alone; she hasn't seen the info snackbar so she missed the first click; then she managed to search a product; she liked the design and found the results easily but it took some time to expand them; she opened very fast a product page and reviewed the result easily; the app is easy to use;

Potential Design Improvements

  • Auto-expand the results.
  • Currency convertor
