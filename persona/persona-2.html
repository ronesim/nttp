Main Points

  • Buys only from malls and hipermarkets
  • No online presence; likes to be incognito
  • Family with 2 kids

Goals
  • Quality over quantity

  • Avoid shopping stress

Frustrations and Pain Points
  • Does no like social media
  • Not up to date with the new technology.
  • Types slowly
Detailed Description

Daniel is an introverted person and he loves spending time with his family (he has 2 little kids). He is not a big fan of technology because he thinks that you can be manipulated very easily nowadays.

Moreover, he is not present on social media and he does not have too many friends

He does not enjoy shopping at all and when he must buy something, he prefers going to hipermarkets or malls.

He has never bought something online because he wants to avoid sharing his personal details like credit card, address, etc

References
Scenarios

If Daniel manages to install the chrome extensions, probably he won't create an account because he does not like to share personal data. If he decides to sign up, he will drop the app because he is not used to shop online.

Type: Served

Name: Bogdan Tarcau

Background
  • Date of Birth: 15/08/1995
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: Freiburg, Germany
  • Work place: Student
  • Technology Level:high
Main Points
  • Experience with online shopping (owns an online store)
  • He is a dropshipper (buy cheap, sell expensive)
  • He does not like price comparison tools
  • Very sociable and friendly
  • Very persuasive
  • Experience with online marketing
Goals
  • Creating an international brand
  • Becoming a billionaire
Frustrations and Pain Points
  • Hates price comparison tools because you can find cheaper alternative for his products.
  • Marketing is too expensive
  • Hard to find what products to sell
  • Tries to modify product images and description in order to avoid detection.
Detailed Description

Bogdan is a small entrepreneur who owns an online store. He is practicing dropshipping: he buys very cheap from china and sells very expensive in US or Europe. Basically he does nothing except marketing. He built an website and a logo and now he is promoting his products.

He does not like price comparison tools because it is very easy to find cheaper alternative for his products. In order to avoid them, he tries to modify the name,description and even the image for each product.

Bogdan is very good at marketing. He managed to create an online store which has over 500000 followers on social media. He has a big traffic on his website and his profit is around 2000 dollars per month.

References
Scenarios

Bogdan won't use the product directly, but he is going to be affected by it. By using our chrome extension, an user can find the real source (most of the time a website from China) for Bogdan's products. His bussiness is goind to be affected because customers won't trust him anymore.
